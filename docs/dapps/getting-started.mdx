# Leverage Smart Wallets

Leverage existing Smart Wallets to improve your dapp experience 

Our developer tooling leverages the power of every smart wallet seamlessly, eliminating the need for special integrations for individual wallets.

With a unified approach, developers can effortlessly tap into the features of any ERC-4337 compatible smart wallet, streamlining development and ensuring a consistent experience for users across the entire ecosystem.
